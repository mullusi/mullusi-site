Boundary map

Public routes expose claims, not private internals.

Mullusi is the umbrella. Mullu, Proof, Playground, Sciences, and Surfaces are public routes. Product engines, runtime internals, repositories, dashboards, and operational data stay private until their release gates close.

  • Public pages describe route boundaries, evidence state, and release posture.
  • Private incubation work is named only as a boundary, not as an accessible surface.
  • Rollback path: point the manifest back to the homepage and remove this route from sitemap/status/build allowlists.

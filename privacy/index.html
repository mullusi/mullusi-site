Privacy.

Mullusi.com is a static public website. It publishes product, proof, status, and contact information. It does not claim a live account system, backend intake store, or runtime product data processor on the public site.

This page applies to the public website and contact routes. Runtime product privacy terms will be separate when those products open.

Local Display choices stay in your browser.

Theme, language, and local visit values are browser-side preferences for rendering the public website.

User sent Email exists only when you send it.

Contact messages are created by your email client and delivered through the mailbox you choose.

Public files Static pages and JSON are public content.

Product, status, news, and language files are published site data, not private customer records.

Reserved Product-service data handling is not open yet.

Runtime product privacy terms and support paths publish before customer access opens.

Local storage

The homepage may store theme, language, and local visit count values in your browser. These values are local to your device and are not site-wide analytics.

Contact

Mail links open your email client. The message is handled through the mailbox you choose, such as hello@mullusi.com, research@mullusi.com, or support@mullusi.com.

Public data

The site serves static public JSON for products, status, proof boundary, news, and localized display text. These files do not contain private customer records.

Runtime products

Runtime product services are not represented as live public data processors here. Their privacy terms require separate publication before customer access opens.

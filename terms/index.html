A public website visit is not a production runtime contract, support commitment, managed service contract, or proof-stamp warranty.

Website content

The website is a public information surface. Content may change as routes publish, statuses update, or older statements are replaced.

Playground

The playground is a deterministic browser simulation. It contacts no server, evaluates no real customer action, and issues no live proof stamp.

Runtime product boundary

Use of runtime services, pilots, dashboards, or private operators requires separate written approval and may require additional product terms.

Evidence state

Claims marked AwaitingEvidence are intentionally unresolved until the named witness paths or public proof records close.

Use rules

  1. Do not use the public site to probe, disrupt, or bypass reserved surfaces.
  2. Do not represent simulated playground output as a live Mullusi proof stamp.
  3. Do not copy public content in a way that removes its proof-state boundary.
  4. Do not submit confidential material through ordinary email unless you accept that mailbox boundary.
  5. Use the responsible disclosure route for suspected security issues.

How it works

From request to reviewed result.

  • A work request is turned into a proposed action with context and visible status.
  • Important steps can be reviewed before execution and recorded afterward.
  • Teams keep the reason, result, repair notes, and history attached to the work.
  • The proof route remains available for the technical runtime boundary.

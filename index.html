What We Build

Symbolic Intelligence

MULLUSI is a product studio focused on symbolic intelligence — software that captures structure, not statistics.

While the industry chases probabilistic models, we build systems that are bounded, inspectable, and verifiable. Our software doesn't guess. It observes, remembers, and exposes what it knows.

We believe the next generation of intelligent tools won't replace human judgment. They'll make the structure of complex information visible, persistent, and queryable.

How We Think

Philosophy

The dominant paradigm in software intelligence is statistical: large models trained on large data making probabilistic guesses. We take a different path.

Symbolic intelligence is older than neural networks. It's the tradition of structured representation, explicit reasoning, and verifiable computation. It fell out of fashion not because it failed, but because it was hard and the hardware wasn't ready.

The hardware is ready now. And the limitations of probabilistic systems — hallucination, opacity, unbounded resource consumption — are becoming clear.

01

Bounded by construction

If it can explode, it will. We design limits in, not bolt them on.

02

Observable at all times

If you can't inspect it, you can't trust it.

03

Deterministic where possible

Same input should produce same output.

04

No inference without consent

We capture structure. We don't guess meaning.

05

Ship when ready

We don't announce roadmaps. We ship certified systems.

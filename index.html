The Shift

Old way vs Mullusi way

Mullusi governs high-risk symbolic intelligence and software actions before they execute. It does not replace model output; it governs whether output-derived actions execute, and re-governs material consequences when context, authority, risk, or dependency state changes.

Judgment Kernel

Judgment kernel

Ψ(PS, SE, EFF, SG, PRR, CPM, ERL, PCE, PCB, K) → J***

Website claims follow the same discipline as the system: observable public surface, stated boundary, reversible update, and no silent failure. That is why the public graph separates deployed surfaces, private incubation, staged domains, and operational interfaces.

In plain terms: Ψ is the judgment step. It releases a public claim only when state, effects, safety guards, proof, and lineage all hold. If anything is unresolved, nothing is published.

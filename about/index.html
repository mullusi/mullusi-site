About Mullusi

Work systems for decisions that need review.

Mullusi builds symbolic intelligence software for teams that need help turning intent into planned, reviewed, and recorded work.

The goal is simple: make complex work easier to coordinate without losing ownership, context, or a clear record of what happened.

Company Focus
